Where to Stay in Taniti

Comfortable, regulated lodging options for every travel style

Home Itineraries Things to Do Getting Around Where to Stay Travel Tips

Taniti offers a wide range of lodging options, all of which are strictly regulated and regularly inspected by the Tanitian government. Whether you prefer a full-service resort or a locally owned hotel, you can expect clean, safe, and well-maintained accommodations.

Hotels

A popular choice for first-time visitors who want comfort and convenience.

Family-Owned Hotels

Affordable and centrally located, often near Taniti City.

Best for: First-time travelers, families

Four-Star Resort

Full-service resort with dining, excursions, and concierge services.

Best for: Relaxed, all-in-one stays

Bed & Breakfasts

Smaller, locally operated lodging with a personal touch.

Beachside B&Bs

Casual stays with ocean views and breakfast included.

Best for: Couples, slower-paced trips

Residential B&Bs

Located in quieter neighborhoods near Taniti City.

Best for: Travelers seeking a calm environment

Vacation Rentals

Ideal for travelers who want more space or longer stays.

Private Condos

Often located near Merriton Landing.

Best for: Couples, repeat visitors

Private Homes

Available throughout the island.

Best for: Extended stays, larger groups

Budget Options

Affordable accommodations for cost-conscious travelers.

Hostel

Basic lodging with shared facilities.

Best for: Solo travelers, short stays

Most first-time visitors choose lodging in or near Taniti City for easy access to beaches, restaurants, and transportation.
