These example itineraries are designed to help you plan your visit to Taniti with confidence. Each option balances sightseeing, relaxation, and flexibility, so you can adjust based on your interests and energy level.

3-Day Itinerary: Highlights of Taniti

Ideal for short stays or cruise visitors who want a taste of the island.

Day 1: Arrival & Taniti City

  • Arrive via airport or cruise dock at Yellow Leaf Bay
  • Check into lodging near Taniti City or Merriton Landing
  • Walk along the beach and explore local shops
  • Dinner at a local seafood restaurant

Day 2: Rainforest Adventure

  • Guided rainforest hike or zip-lining excursion
  • Lunch in Merriton Landing
  • Optional snorkeling or fishing charter
  • Relaxed evening or movie night

Day 3: Volcano & Departure

  • Morning visit to Taniti’s active volcano
  • Stop at the local history museum
  • Souvenir shopping before departure

5-Day Itinerary: Explore & Unwind

A balanced schedule for travelers who want both adventure and downtime.

Days 1–2: Beaches & City Life

  • Beach time around Yellow Leaf Bay
  • Explore Taniti City architecture and markets
  • Visit pubs or microbrewery in the evening

Day 3: Rainforest & Zip-Lining

  • Half-day guided rainforest tour
  • Lunch at Merriton Landing
  • Optional arcade or art gallery visit

Day 4: Volcano & Island Tour

  • Bus or boat tour of the island
  • Volcano viewing and scenic overlooks
  • Evening at leisure

Day 5: Free Day & Departure

  • Shopping, snorkeling, or golf (when available)
  • Pack and depart

7-Day Itinerary: The Complete Taniti Experience

Best for travelers who prefer a slower pace and deeper exploration.

Days 1–3: Settle In

  • Beaches, walking tours, and local dining
  • Visit museums and cultural sites

Days 4–5: Adventure & Nature

  • Rainforest hike and zip-lining
  • Snorkeling or fishing charter
  • Optional helicopter ride

Days 6–7: Relax & Reflect

  • Free exploration or spa day
  • Final shopping and beachfront dining
  • Departure

All itineraries are flexible. Public transportation, taxis, and rentals make it easy to adjust plans based on weather, energy level, or local holidays.
