Things to Do in Taniti

Explore at your own pace — adventure, culture, and relaxation

Home Itineraries Things to Do Getting Around Where to Stay Travel Tips

Taniti offers a variety of activities suited for both relaxed travelers and those looking for adventure. Most attractions are easily accessible from Taniti City or Merriton Landing, making it simple to mix sightseeing with downtime.

Nature & Outdoor Adventures

Ideal for travelers who want to experience Taniti’s natural landscape.

Beaches & Snorkeling

White-sand beaches surrounding Yellow Leaf Bay.

Intensity: Low

Location: Taniti City

Rainforest Hiking

Guided and self-guided trails through tropical rainforest.

Intensity: Moderate

Location: Interior of island

Zip-Lining

Guided zip-line tours above the rainforest canopy.

Intensity: Moderate–High

Duration: Half-day

Volcano Tours

Safe, guided visits to Taniti’s active volcano.

Intensity: Moderate

Best Time: Morning

Culture & History

Slower-paced activities that provide context and insight into Taniti.

Local History Museum

Learn about Taniti’s indigenous culture and history.

Time Needed: 1–2 hours

Art Galleries

Featuring work by local artists.

Location: Taniti City

Architecture Walks

Explore traditional Tanitian buildings and neighborhoods.

Intensity: Low

Entertainment & Nightlife

Casual, low-pressure entertainment options in the evenings.

Pubs & Microbrewery

Relaxed nightlife with local and imported drinks.

Alcohol Sales: Until midnight

Dance Club

Newly opened club in Merriton Landing.

Best For: Late evenings

Movie Theater

English-language films available.

Bowling & Arcade

Indoor activities for families or rainy days.

Guided Tours & Experiences

Structured experiences for visitors who prefer expert guidance.

Boat Tours

Scenic tours around Yellow Leaf Bay.

Fishing Charters

Half-day and full-day guided fishing trips.

Helicopter Rides

Aerial views of the island and volcano.

Optional Experience

Many activities are located near Merriton Landing or Taniti City, allowing travelers to combine multiple experiences in one day.
