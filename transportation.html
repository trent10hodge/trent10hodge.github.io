Which Option Is Best for You?

  • Walking: Staying in Taniti City or Merriton Landing
  • Buses: Budget-friendly travel during the day
  • Taxis: Evening travel or guided outings
  • Rental Car: Exploring the island independently

Most first-time visitors find that a combination of walking and taxis is sufficient for a comfortable stay.
